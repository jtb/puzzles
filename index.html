Welcome to my puzzles page

Hello! My name is Justin, and this is my puzzle collection (and a few non-puzzles). I gravitate more towards vintage puzzles, and enjoy discovering the history behind the puzzles. You can tell a lot of my tastes by what puzzles I have the most. This is my attempt to catalog all my puzzles, in hopes that others might find it useful as well. I've learned a lot from others' puzzle websites and enjoy talking with other puzzle enthusiasts. This site is spoiler free - you should be able to browse, without accidentally getting any hints to how to solve the puzzles. I am starting a blog section as well, where I might go into some more details on a puzzle, but I'll be sure to warn of any spoilers. Happy puzzling!
